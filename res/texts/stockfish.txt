Stockfish is a chess engine, means that it can automatically
play chess against you. It is free and open source, developed
by T. Romstad, M. Costalba, J. Kiiski, G. Linscott and many
others.

Regarded as one of the best chess engine in the world, it is
very popular among people.
Check out its homepage at stockfishchess.org, source code
is also available at github.

My-PyChess is meant to act like a GUI and provide a medium
for you to play against it.
